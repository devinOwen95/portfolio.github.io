About me

A little bit about me.

My name is Devin Owen and I am currently a student of Eleven Fifty Academy. I am an aspiring Junior Web Developer, film-maker, story writer, and currently a CNC machinist.

My intial interest in web development came with my love of travel. I wanted to find a way to work remotely, so I could travel the world, while still being a functioning member of society. Web Devlopment seemed liked a good, and well paying, option. Soon money become a second factor as I discovered my love of coding.
Along with my love of coding, my other intrests include traveling, story-writing, and film-making. I have traveled to four great countries, and multiple states. My favorite being Thailand. My love of writing is primarily include screenplays, though I have written a fair share of short stories as well.

My skills include HTML, CSS, JAVA-SCRIPT, WRITING, FILMING, EDITING, CNC MACHINERY, and MANUAL MILL.
